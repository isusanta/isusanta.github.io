## Research Appointments

Center for Computational Life Sciences (CLS). Research spans quantum computing for electronic structure and molecular energetics (EWF-SQD pipeline for FLiBe molten salt clusters on IBM quantum hardware; ORNL project CHM243) and QM/ML methods for metabolomics and precision medicine.

---

**Postdoctoral Fellow** | Michigan State University, Department of Chemistry | East Lansing, Michigan, USA | Apr 2019 – Oct 2023

Supervisor: Prof. Kenneth M. Merz Jr. Research areas: QM/ML spectroscopy workflows for metabolomics (NMR chemical shift and collisional cross section prediction); AutoGraph conformational clustering algorithm; web server development for metabolomics characterization (pomics.org).
